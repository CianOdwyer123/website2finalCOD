Events


Through all 12 months of the year, there is many events that take place in Dunboyne.

All these are very different and very enjoyable by all of the community.

A couple of these events are:

The Spirit of Dunboyne: Motor Club Show:

"The Spirit of Dunboyne: Motor Club Show" takes place every year on the 28th and 29th of September. During this event, many people gather to showcase their vintage, famous and expensive vehicles. Their is also plenty of stalls for everyone.

These stalls include

  • Hook a Duck
  • Food
  • Drink
  • Simulator
  • Ballon Artist

Dunboyne Youth Musical Society (DYMS) Christmas Show:

Every year, a group people from ages 7 to 20 put on a famous musical just before Christmas for the entire Community. This is a great fundraiser, as they get money from tickets, food and raffles. Every single year they out do themselves.

The Dunboyne St Patricks Day Parade:

Every single Year, a week before St Paddys Day, Dunboyne have their own individual parade to celebrate St Patricks Day. During this parade they have many floats.

On these floats there is:

  • Irish Dancers
  • Singers
  • Acrobats
  • Shops
  • Scouts
